Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

GramAI is built privacy-first. This policy explains what data is — and is not — collected when you use the GramAI browser extension and website.

Local mode (Free tier)

When you use GramAI in local mode with Ollama:

  • No text leaves your device. All AI processing happens on your computer.
  • No account required. We do not ask for your name, email, or password.
  • No analytics on your writing. We do not read, log, or train on your content.
  • History is stored locally in your browser via Chrome storage APIs. You can clear it anytime.

Pro Cloud mode

When you use Pro Cloud with a license key:

  • Text you submit for processing is sent to our cloud server over HTTPS.
  • Requests are processed in real time and immediately discarded.
  • We do not log, store, or use your text for model training.
  • Your license key and email (from purchase) are stored to validate your subscription.

Website & waitlist

Our landing page at gramai-extension.vercel.app may use:

  • Plausible Analytics — privacy-friendly, cookieless page view stats. No personal data sold.
  • Email waitlist — if you opt in, we store your email to notify you about Chrome Web Store launch. Unsubscribe anytime via support@gramai.app.
  • GitHub API — we fetch public star counts. No user data is sent.

Payments

Pro and Team subscriptions are processed by LemonSqueezy. We do not store your credit card details. See LemonSqueezy's privacy policy.

Third-party AI

  • Local mode: Uses Ollama on your machine. See ollama.com for their policies.
  • Pro Cloud: May use OpenAI API when configured by the server operator. Text is not retained by GramAI after processing.

Data we do not collect

  • Keystroke logging or full-page content scraping
  • Browsing history outside of text you explicitly select
  • Selling or sharing data with advertisers
  • Cross-site tracking cookies on the extension

Open source

GramAI is open source under the MIT License. You can audit the code at github.com/TRUSHAKHACHARIYA/gramai-extension.

Children

GramAI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be noted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

Questions? Email support@gramai.app.
